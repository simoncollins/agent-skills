# Discovery Questionnaire Template

Use this structure when creating the Phase 1 questionnaire. Adapt questions to the specific topic.

---

## Core Thesis

**What's the single sentence you want readers to walk away with?**

>

**Is this primarily a prediction, a prescription, or a warning?**
(Or combination - what's the dominant tone?)

>

---

## Audience

**Who is this for?**
- [ ] Software engineers
- [ ] Business leaders/executives
- [ ] Product managers
- [ ] General public
- [ ] Other:

**What's the reader's current mental model that you're trying to shift?**
(What do they believe now that you're arguing is wrong?)

>

---

## The Stakes

**What happens to someone who ignores this?**
(Be specific - concrete downside)

>

**What does the world look like in [X years] if you're right?**

>

---

## Key Claims

For each major claim in the outline, ask:

**Claim: "[Insert claim]"**
- Can you give a concrete example?
- What does this look like in practice?
- Who are you arguing against?

>

---

## Examples & Evidence

**What existing tools/products best represent the direction you're describing?**

>

**Can you describe a specific workflow - current vs. future?**

>

**Have you seen anyone doing this well already?**

>

---

## Objections

**What's the strongest argument against your thesis?**

>

**Are there areas where the opposite might be true?**

>

---

## Style & Tone

**Are there articles/essays whose style you admire for this kind of piece?**

>

**How long should this be?**
- [ ] Short (800-1200 words)
- [ ] Medium (1500-2500 words)
- [ ] Long (3000+ words)

**Where will this be published?**

>

---

## Anything Else

**What didn't I ask about that's important?**

>
